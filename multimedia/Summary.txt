Multimedia summary for RA-L submission: manipulation-entry collapse in safe RL.

Contents:
  multimedia.mp4  — single H.264 video (~90 s, <=120 s limit)
  ReadMe.txt      — playback notes and clip description

Video content:
  Side-by-side robosuite Lift rollouts (Panda): unconstrained SAC-Value prior
  (left) vs. phase-aware constrained fine-tuning M4.1 (right), at object pose
  noise sigma = 0 cm and 6 cm. Matched reset seeds at sigma = 6 cm. Closing
  panel: success, manipulation-entry rate, and P(success | entry).

Usage:
  Play with any standard MP4/H.264 viewer (VLC, QuickTime, browser). No special
  software, login, or hardware required.

Value:
  Complements the main text by showing the behavioral signature of entry
  collapse under shift: constrained policies often hesitate or fail before
  sustained manipulation while the prior still attempts contact-rich grasping.
  Extended tables, methods, and data are in the anonymous supplement repository
  cited in the paper (not in this zip).
